    • Nawazuddin Ahmed


    I am an Assistant Professor at Symbiosis School of Economics, Pune. I did PhD in economics from the Department of Humanities & Social Sciences, IIT Roorkee (India). The title of my thesis is ‘Intergenerational Occupational Mobility Among Socio-Religious Communities in India: With Special Reference to the Muslims’. My research interests lie in the intersection of labour and development economics. I work on Intra-generational and Intergenerational mobility issues, socio-religious and caste-based differences in socio-economic outcomes, and stratification in the labour market.
